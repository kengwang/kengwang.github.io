《大地惊雷》 观后感二改

2020-02-03

This is a film made by Coen brothers, I haven't watched their films before. This may be the first.

The whole film is filled with WILD! The wild shootout, the wild jungle, the wild conversation including the main character Cogburn.

First, is about the main character Cogburn. Form my point of view, he's a real MAN. He is a brave cowhand. The word that he answer the question "You think the one on four is a dogfall?" is "I mean to kill you in one minute" , full of brave and confidence. He had the courage to hit four people, and he almost did it! This part really amazed me ! No fear to face the dangerous situation, never care about the risk of doing everything! He's an old police and a Bounty Hunter. He killed a lot of people.

And the most important part is kind heart. At the last part of the film, the girl fell off in a cave, bitten by a snake. He must send the girl to the Doctor's of a long distance. But he tried to save her, running on a horse with injuries. When the horse tired, he carry the girl for 5 kilometer by himself! Though he is too old to doo these things but he tried his best to carry the girl to the doctor.

The last sentence "I've grown old" is so impressive. Is he old. Yes, his hair is white and hobbled. But his wild heart isn't old.

Second, is about Ross. She is another kind of girl. She (I'm so nerves to use she) said the word like a man. She can have a hard journey to revenge. She talks unlike the word a 14-year-old girl says on her age (Didn't act her age?). She said the word very strong and irresistible. (Is this a kind of wild?)

She's got a big courage (absolutely bigger than me). When Cogburn left her and went on the way to find Chany alone, She rode horse across the river to join the team!

All the words I wanted to say is not just these. About the Music, about the actors, about the west environment...... The truth is, this film is a tradition. It showed us the western America, and also the story of a True Grit.

What is a True Grit? That seems to be an easy but difficult question.
